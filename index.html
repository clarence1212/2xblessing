Book 1: The Memoir - Chapter 1 Preview

Chapter 1: Born Between Altars

"I was born into a house of gods—but not the God I would eventually serve. The youngest of eleven children in 1960s Singapore, I entered a world where ancestral altars competed with modern aspirations, where incense smoke mingled with the dreams of a rapidly changing nation..."

My earliest memories are filled with the acrid smell of joss sticks and the sight of oranges arranged carefully before wooden idols. Every morning, I watched my mother light incense at the altar, her weathered hands moving in practiced motions passed down through generations. She would mutter prayers in Hokkien, asking for protection, prosperity, and favor from ancestors I had never met.

Our shophouse on Pagoda Street stood as a testament to old Singapore—narrow, cramped, and filled with more people than rooms. As the eleventh child, I learned early that space, food, and attention were commodities to be shared, not owned. My siblings and I slept on thin mattresses spread across the floor, our dreams literally overlapping in the humid night air.

But something stirred within me even then, a restlessness that the household gods could not quiet. While my brothers found comfort in the familiar rituals and my sisters prepared to continue the traditions, I found myself asking questions that had no answers in our ancestral worship...

The chapter continues with young Elias's first encounter with Christianity through a neighbor, setting the stage for a lifetime journey from chronos to kairos...

Book 2: Prayer Guidelines - Breaking Generational Patterns

Chapter 3: Breaking the Stronghold of Ancestral Worship

Biblical Foundation:

Exodus 20:3-5 - "You shall have no other gods before me. You shall not make for yourself an image in the form of anything in heaven above or on the earth beneath..."

Galatians 3:13-14 - "Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law by becoming a curse for us... so that in Christ Jesus the blessing of Abraham might come to the Gentiles."

Breaking Prayer:

Heavenly Father, in the mighty name of Jesus, I come before Your throne of grace. I acknowledge that my family line has served other gods and engaged in ancestral worship. Today, I choose to break these chains that have bound my family for generations.

I renounce all covenants, dedications, and agreements made with ancestral spirits, household gods, and territorial deities. I declare that Jesus Christ is my only Lord and Savior. I break every soul tie connecting me to false altars and idolatrous practices...

Blessing Declaration:

I declare that my household is now under the Lordship of Jesus Christ. Where there was spiritual bondage, I now walk in freedom. Where there was generational curse, I now inherit generational blessing. My children and children's children shall know the Lord and serve Him only...

This chapter includes specific prayers for different aspects of ancestral worship, practical steps for removing altars, and testimonies of breakthrough...

Book 3: 365-Day Devotional - Week 1, Day 1

1

Before You Were Born

Theme: Trust in Divine Timing

Today's Scripture:

"Before I formed you in the womb I knew you, before you were born I set you apart; I appointed you as a prophet to the nations." - Jeremiah 1:5 (NIV)

Memoir Reflection:

Born as the youngest of eleven children in 1960s Singapore, surrounded by ancestral altars and incense smoke, I had no idea that God was already writing my name in His book. While my parents dedicated me to household gods, the God of Heaven had already set me apart for His purposes. What seemed like an inauspicious beginning—poverty, idol worship, and family dysfunction—was actually God's perfect setup for a testimony of transformation.

Deeper Dive:

God's timing operates in the realm of kairos, not chronos. While we count days and years, He orchestrates destinies. Your birth circumstances—whether celebrated or challenging—do not catch God by surprise. Like Moses in a basket or Jesus in a manger, God often places His chosen vessels in unlikely beginnings to display His glory more magnificently...

Life Application:

Today, examine your own beginning. What circumstances of your birth or early life have you considered disadvantages? List three specific situations that seemed negative. Now, ask the Holy Spirit to reveal how God might use these very circumstances as preparation for your calling...

Today's Declaration:

"I am not an accident. God formed me with intention and set me apart for His purposes. My beginning is His starting point for blessing upon blessing."

Prayer Focus:

Father, thank You for knowing me before I was formed. I surrender my past, present, and future to Your perfect timing. Transform every disadvantage in my story into a display of Your glory. In Jesus' name, Amen.
